Medical Technologies
We invest continuously in state-of-the-art equipment so our clinicians can diagnose faster and treat more effectively.
3T MRI Scanner
High-field magnetic resonance imaging for detailed soft-tissue diagnosis without radiation.
64-Slice CT Scanner
Rapid, high-resolution cross-sectional imaging for cardiac, vascular, and oncological assessment.
Robotic Surgery System
Minimally invasive robotic-assisted surgery for precision, reduced blood loss, and faster recovery.
Digital Pathology
Whole-slide imaging and AI-assisted analysis for rapid, accurate laboratory diagnosis.
